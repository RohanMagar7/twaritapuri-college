Principal Message:

Welcome to Twaritapuri Arts And Science College, Talwada. This college established in June, 2012. Being the Principal of this college, it is my responsibility to endure the accomplishment of academic excellence to each and every stake holder irrespective of his/her caste, creed, birth, region and religion. I view my role as that of a catalyst to provoke and draw the best out of the inherent and latent potential of the Shahu Parivar. I feel privileged to welcome you in this campus and promise that we will enable you to transform your future tailored as per your dreams.
